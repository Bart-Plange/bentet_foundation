What You Have to Know about Ben Tet Foundation

The Ben Tet Foundation Aid, established several years ago, is a non-profit, non-partisan organization based in Ghana. Our primary mission is to promote and support education, with a special emphasis on children. We are dedicated to offering educational opportunities to children from diverse ethnic backgrounds and races.

Furthermore, our foundation concentrates its efforts on two main areas. Firstly, we are committed to providing essential food aid to underprivileged families. Secondly, we extend support by covering school fees for those in need.

Our ultimate goal is to make a positive impact on the lives of children facing adversity, including those from broken homes, school dropouts, and children who may have lost hope for a brighter future. We aspire to guide them towards a place filled with hope and opportunities.
